Frequent Ask Questions

How Soon Will My Pup Be With Me?

The adoption process usually takes 2–5 days depending on meeting availability.

Is There Any Adoption Fee?

Yes, each pet has a small adoption fee to support shelter operations.

Can I Return My Pet?

Returns are possible only for urgent and valid reasons.

Can I Adopt More Than One?

Yes, you can adopt multiple pets as long as you meet the requirement.

How Do I Take Care Of My Pet?

We provide a guidebook after adoption to help you care for your new friend.
